"Our solution involving the gender issues of Ancient China"

         Problem: "Gender Issues in ancient china civilization"

During Ancient China, living and being born as a female was a struggle in the country. They were politically,socially and economically challenged. With cultures and traditions that make a bad image on women, stereotypes are widely done in the country. They are heavily affected by the male-dominated society.


          Solution: "Cultural Change involving women empowerment"

Having a change of culture with the involvement of empowerment of women will help or solve the issue involving the social,political and economic issues that involve the women of Ancient China. With a solution like this, the gender stereotypes that women are receiving will lessened. Empowerment will also help make the people realize how women can be good or better in terms of political and social issues. Moving closer to where women's voices are heard and where their full potential talents are recognized. This cultural shift will eventually clear the way for a more equal and forward-looking society in ancient China.

We can change this by having campaigns that support the idea of cultural change with the involvement of women empowerment. We can also make documentaries or articles that helps spread the idea of this solution that we have composed of
